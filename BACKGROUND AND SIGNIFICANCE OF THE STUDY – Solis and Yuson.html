OUR BACKGROUND AND SIGNIFICANCE OF THE STUDY

The goal is to make sure that every child receives at least one nutritious meal per day. Food for Children and Nuns provides assistance and food to children that truly need help. That means we fund feeding programs for SMAC sisters. We seek out those children care programs that have exhibited the ability and history to care effectively for the needs of children, yet struggle to provide the necessary food. From the children's care providers, we require project financial accountability, monthly reports, updates on the condition of the orphans, and unlimited access to the project.

The data contributed to our partner institution( SMAC sisters) allows them to grow more confidences and empower them from the positive aspects presented in said data . Overall the data shows their professionalism and how they can look at needs that they need to serve and also how they serve those needs, with data of how they serve the children even if they cannot because of the pandemic or data that shows them doing more for the community. When looking at an outside perspective this shows their resilience being able to do these things even through tough times but also boost them from this knowledge.

The general public can benefit from this in diverse ways. With a food drive constantly dispensing food, the orphaned have to rely less on donors and are much less restricted in terms of food. A food bank can also help its community by better convincing parents unable or unwilling to take care of their children to hand them over to the orphanage and ultimately give them better living conditions than otherwise. That and, giving orphans better living conditions is invariably a good thing.

The project is important to us student because it gives us an opportunity to serve as well as help the ones in need. The project has posed many challenges but overall has been manageable, but with that the project has given us the opportunity to help the ones in need as well as serve, this opportunity giving us experience as well as wisdom. Overall the opportunity presents us with experience and wisdom
